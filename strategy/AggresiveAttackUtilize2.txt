Aggresive Greedy Attack

Collect energy strategy
- Objective: Get energy per turn above 27 (9 building energy) with asumption in every turn we can always build attack or defence building
- Constraint: Have minimum 20 energy
- Solution: Place energy building randomly from back

Defence strategy
- Objective: Place defense in row where there is four missiles in that row
- Constraint: Have minimum 30 energy
- Solution: Place defense tower in those row

Attack strategy
- Objective: Aggresive attacking, place in row there the ninth energy tower not in that row and
there is at most 1 defense tower and at least one attack tower
- Constraint: Have minimum 30 energy 
- Solution: Place attack tower in those row
